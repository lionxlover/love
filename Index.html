Prem Kya Hai?

Prem sirf ek ehsaas nahi, ek poora sansaar hai. Yeh woh anmol rishta hai jo dil se nikalkar dil tak pahunchta hai. Prem mein na koi shartein hoti hain, na hi koi apeksha... bas ek sacchai hoti hai.

Psychological Insight

Research shows that love activates the same brain regions as cocaine, creating feelings of euphoria. True love (prem) goes beyond this initial high to create deep, lasting bonds through oxytocin and vasopressin release.

Love vs Attachment

Psychology distinguishes between love (selfless care) and attachment (emotional dependence). True prem is about giving without expectation, while attachment seeks security.

Unrequited Love

Studies show unrequited love activates brain regions associated with motivation and reward, explaining its persistence. Bhakti (devotion) transforms this into spiritual energy.

Chemistry vs Commitment

Attraction is dopamine-driven (excitement), while love is oxytocin-driven (bonding). Prem combines both with the depth of spiritual connection.

Healing Heartbreak

Research suggests heartbreak activates the same brain regions as physical pain. Mindfulness and self-compassion are scientifically proven healing methods.
